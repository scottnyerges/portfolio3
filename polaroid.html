artwork

One of the sounds I most closely associate with my childhood is the mechanical buzz of the Polaroid SX-70. For the past couple years, I've been using instant cameras like the Land SX-70 as visual sketchbook, framing the rare gems and using the castoffs as the raw ingredients of collage. Sometimes, I pair them with portions of black-and-white film prints, uniting my two favorite photo media. The process is tactile, kinetic, and a tonic for too many hours spent in front of a computer.
